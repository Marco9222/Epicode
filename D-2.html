Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped withsweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become yournew favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons
  • 1 3/4 cups

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow
  • 1 tablaspoon
  • 1-2 tablespoons

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
